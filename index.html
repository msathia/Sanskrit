महेश्वरसूत्राणि

The sound-map behind compact grammar terms such as अच्, हल्, इक्, and यण्.

Maheshwara Sutras

अच् = all vowels
हल् = all consonants
हलन्तशब्दः = consonant-ending word
  • १४ महेश्वरसूत्राणि
  • हल् starts at ह and ends at the final ल् marker
  • यण् = य् व् र् ल्

शतृ-शानच्

Present participle forms: verb-derived words such as “singing” or “while doing.”

Participles From लट्लकारः

ति-ending forms -> often शतृ
ते-ending forms -> often शानच्
  • शतृ and शानच् form present participles
  • Use the verb ending to choose the प्रत्ययः
  • शतृ and शानच् notes
